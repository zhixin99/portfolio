About Me Hello, I’m Zhixin, a frontend developer with a strategic background in marketing.

Get to know me!

My journey into tech started at a MedTech startup in Singapore. While my title was in marketing, I found myself more drawn to the Shopify setup and tracking pixels than the marketing campaigns themselves. I loved the immediate impact of fixing a bug or optimizing a landing page to improve the user experience.
When I discovered the Netherlands’ Search Year visa, I saw a unique opportunity to turn this passion into a career. With savings from my first job, I made the decision to take a deliberate pause and reassess my long-term direction.
Over the past few months, I have completed the Frontend Developer Career Path on Scrimba, built several solo projects, and received encouraging feedback from the community. The experience confirmed that I enjoy the process of building — understanding how systems work, structuring code, and turning ideas into working products.
I am now looking for opportunities where I can continue growing as a developer, contribute thoughtfully to a team, and build products that are both technically sound and genuinely useful.
